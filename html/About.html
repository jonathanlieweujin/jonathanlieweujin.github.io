Who Are We?
We are Jeng Sushi, a sushi restaurant that has been thriving in Malaysia since 2000. Our specialty is handcrafted sushi, and our founder, Chef Yeap, is not only the owner but also the strategic mind behind our success.
So, why did Chef Yeap create Jeng Sushi?
Faced with the challenge of finding a high-quality hand-made sushi restaurant offering reasonable prices, Chef Yeap, both a Malaysian and a chef, made a pivotal decision. He resolved to establish a dependable sushi restaurant where patrons could savor their meals without fretting over costs. Thus, Jeng Sushi was born, with its first outlet in Kampar. This culinary endeavor was brought to life with limited resources and a dedicated team consisting of Chef Yeap and his skilled disciples.
At Jeng Sushi, we are committed to providing a delightful sushi experience that's accessible to all, a vision that has been at the core of our journey from the very beginning.
Mission
Our mission is to serve with pride and work with passion to uphold the promise, delivered with unwavering dedication and excellence.
Vision
Our vision is to become the foremost destination for traditional hand-pressed sushi in Malaysia, all while maintaining affordability.
CORE VALUES
Our core values serve as the foundation of our identity. We believe in the importance of respect, not only for ourselves but also for our valued customers and the dedicated JENG Team. Passion drives our commitment to our duty, propelling us toward continuous learning and knowledge sharing. With courage, we face challenges and embrace failures as opportunities to restart and grow. Above all, we uphold the principles of never giving up, perpetually seeking knowledge, and consistently treating others with respect.
